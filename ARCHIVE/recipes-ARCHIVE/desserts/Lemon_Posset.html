Lemon Posset

Summer â€¢ Lemon

Ingredients

  • 6 lemons (halves long ways)
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 1.5 cups heavy whipping cream
  • Â¾ cup sugar
  • Â¼ + 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • Zest 1 lemon

Instructions

1. Spoon out the pulp from the lemons and press through a sieve so the juice is extracted.

2. Split vanilla pod, scrape the seeds, and mash with a little sugar. Add vanilla sugar and the rest of the sugar into a pan with the whipping cream. Stir and heat for about 5 minutes.

3. Remove pan from heat and add 6.5 tbsp of lemon juice and zest. Pour mixture into lemon halves and refrigerate overnight.

4. Serve cold!
