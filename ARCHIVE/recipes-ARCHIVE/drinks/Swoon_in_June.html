Swoon in June (Blackberry Smash/Whiskey Sour)

Autumn • Bourbon

Ingredients

  • Fresh mint
  • 6 blackberries
  • ¼ oz simple syrup
  • ½ oz sherry
  • ¾ oz lemon juice
  • 1.5 oz Bourbon
  • 1 egg white
  • Mint crown (garnish)

Instructions

1. Muddle mint and blackberries, then add simple syrup.

2. Add sherry, lemon juice, bourbon, and egg white. Dry shake.

2. Then add ice, wet shake, and double strain into a chilled coupe glass. Top with a crown of mint.
