Raspberry Crumble Muffins

Raspberry • Baked Good • Muffin

Ingredients

  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • ⅔ cups + 2 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ½ cup greek yogurt
  • 1 cup AP flour
  • ¾ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ¾ cup raspberries (fresh or frozen)
  • Streusel: ⅓ cup flour, ¼ cup sugar, ⅛ cup cold butter, pinch of salt

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix streusel ingredients into crumbly dough and place in refrigerator.

2. Whisk egg, sugar, zest, and salt for about 3 minutes. Then add oil and butter and mix until combined.

3. Add yogurt and vanilla and mix again. Add flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Fold in.

4. Gently fold in raspberries. Scoop batter into prepared muffin tins and top with streusel mixture.

5. Bake for about 20 minutes
