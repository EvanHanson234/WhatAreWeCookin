Espresso Fudge Brownies

Espresso • Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp espresso
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup AP flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 bar dark chocolate (roughly chopped)
  • Flaky sea salt (garnish)

Instructions

1. Combine melted butter, sugars, salt, espresso, and vanilla. Then whisk in eggs.

2. Fold in cocoa powder and flour. Then add chocolate pieces.

3. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Top with flaky salt.
