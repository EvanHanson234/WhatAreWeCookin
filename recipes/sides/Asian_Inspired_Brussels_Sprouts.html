Asian Inspired Brussels Sprouts

Vegetarian â€¢ Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • 1 package of brussels sprouts
  • Kewpie mayo
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Furikake Seasoning
  • Black sesame seeds
  • Sesame oil

Instructions

1. Pre heat oven to broil and place rack on bottom setting.

2. Half and clean each sprout, toss in sesame oil and salt, then place face down on baking sheet.

3. Broil for 15 minutes.

4. Plate and top with drizzle of kewpie mayo, hoisin sauce, furikake seasoning, and black sesame seeds.
