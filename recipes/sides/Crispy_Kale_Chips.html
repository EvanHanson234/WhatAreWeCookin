Crispy Kale Chips

Vegetarian • Kale

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of kale
  • Garlic salt to taste

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees while you clean and derib the kale.

2. Chop into chip-size pieces and spray with mild oil. Massage oil into the kale.

3. Season with garlic salt and cook for 18 minutes.
