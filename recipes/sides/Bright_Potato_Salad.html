Bright Potato Salad

Spring • Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb baby yellow potatoes
  • 6 oz mozzarella pearls
  • 1 red bell pepper (minced)
  • ½ red onion (minced)
  • 1 bunch of cilantro (minced)
  • 3 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ranch powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

1. In a large saucepan, crisp up the baby potatoes in some olive oil. When browned add 4 cups to water and salt until water evaporates.

2. Once cool, slice into rounds. Add in mozzarella, onion, pepper, and cilantro.

3. Combine oil, vinegar, mustard, and spices to create a vinaigrette. Toss the salad and top with more black pepper!
