Pulled Tofu Burger

Vegetarian ‚Ä¢ Tofu

Ingredients

  • 2 blocks firm tofu (dried)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp chicken bouillon/chicken spice
  • 4 Burger buns
  • Burger toppings to taste
  • Tofu Sauce: 1 onion (chopped), 2 cloves garlic (chopped), olive oil, 1 tbsp sriracha, 3 tbsp ketchup, 2 tbsp each soy sauce, vinegar, and agave

Instructions

1. Toss chicken in spices, oil, soy sauce, and salt to taste. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Stirring occasionally..

2. Meanwhile, combine ingredients for tofu sauce on the stove, adding water to thin as needed. Cook until gently bubbling while toasting burger buns in some butter.

3. Stir in tofu into sauce and assemble burgers.
