  • 2 cups spinach (roughly chopped)
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon

Instructions

1. Cook tortellini according to package instructions. Add butter and olive oil to a large pan on medium low heat.

2. Saute garlic, pepper, red pepper flakes for about a minute before adding cream and simmering for 3 minutes.

3. Once reduced, slowly add in lemon juice, stirring constantly. Then add zest and parmesan cheese.

4. Add in spinach, tortellini, and some pasta water. Season additionally to taste.

5. Serve with black pepper, extra lemon zest, parmesan cheese, and basil.
