Coconut Curry Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • Block of tofu (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce (2 for tofu)
  • Container of mushrooms
  • 1 package of rice noodles
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • Spices to taste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • 1 carrot (shredded)
  • 1 lime
  • Cilantro (for garnish)
  • Chopped peanuts (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Coat tofu in flour, then stir in soy sauce and olive oil. Fry in a nonstick pan. (Flip until each side is golden).
  2. Saute mushrooms until tender. Add in tomato paste, spices, and coconut milk.
  3. Let that simmer for a bit before adding peanut butter, maple syrup, water, carrots, and noodles.
  4. Let that reduce before mixing in some cilantro and half of the tofu.
  5. Top with tofu, carrots, peanuts, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.
