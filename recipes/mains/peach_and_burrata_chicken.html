Peach and Burrata Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 Peaches (chopped)
  • 1 bunch fresh basil (chopped)
  • 1 bunch fresh chives (chopped)
  • 1 shallot (finely minced)
  • Chopped almonds
  • Chicken breast
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Dijon mustard
  • Honey (spoonful)
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions

  1. Macerate the shallot in a couple tablespoons of champagne vinegar (or ACV).
  2. Combine balsamic, sugar, olive oil, dijon mustard, and salt (all to taste) to create a marinade.
  3. Cut chicken nice and thin then add to bag with marinade and allow to sit for a few hours.
  4. Grill chicken on grill or cast iron pan and finish in the oven. Combine peaches, herbs, shallot, chopped almonds, and honey. Add olive oil and salt to taste.
  5. Top chickens with pulled burrata, peach mixture, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.
