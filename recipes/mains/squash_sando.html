Squash Sando

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Tomato (sliced)
  • Mozzarella
  • Chicken breast
  • Zucchini (Ribbons)
  • Pesto
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Good bread

Instructions

  1. Toast bread and saute zucchini in some lemon, garlic, salt, pepper, and whatever else is lying around until tender.
  2. Spread pesto on both slices of bread and drizzle some balsamic glaze on top. Assemble!
